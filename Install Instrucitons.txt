Install Instrictions:

-Open A Clockwork Project Master folder 

-Then open the 7 Days To Die folder

-Copy and Paste the 7DaysToDie_Data, Data and Mods folders into your 7 Days To Die folder most commonly found here:

C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\7 Days To Die

Merge, Copy and replace everything when prompted!
